Operationalize Everpure Data Intelligence at the moment of action: a low-latency context, recommendation, and evidence service for the platforms that govern AI agents.

The shift

Agents are moving from answering to acting, and trust in actions is now the bottleneck.

As enterprise AI moves from supervised copilots to autonomous execution, model capability alone becomes a less durable source of differentiation.

  • The critical control point shifts to the context governing each action: who is acting, what authority they have over the data involved, and what the risk of the proposed action is.
  • The buying question is no longer "is the model smart enough." It is "can I let it touch my data."

The gap

Every high-risk agent action needs an answer nobody serves today: what data does this touch, and what happens if it is wrong?

  • Identity platforms know who or what is acting, and under whose authority.
  • Policy engines know the rules the customer wrote.
  • Nobody serves cross enterprise 360° data context at decision time: sensitivity, ownership, lineage, business criticality, and recoverability.
  • That context exists, but it is fragmented across catalogs, security tools, and storage, and it is not delivered in a form authorization systems can consume.

Go to market

Sell through the installed base, but price and fund it as its own business.

  • Start with 2 to 3 paid design partners, each integrating one high-risk workflow with an agent runtime, MCP gateway, or authorization platform.
  • The 14,500-customer base opens doors. Specialist AI and data-security sellers own discovery and the technical sale; storage account executives get quota credit.
  • Price on the scope of risk governed, not storage capacity: platform fee plus tiers on protected workflows or governed data domains.

The standalone test

Independent security, AI-platform, or cyber-resilience budget is the test of whether this is a standalone business. If it only sells through storage budgets, it is a feature, not a business.

Metrics, falsification, and risk

We will know within two quarters if we are wrong, and we know what we will do about it.

North-star metric

Monthly production high-risk agent actions governed using a unique Everpure context signal.

  • 30% reduction in severity-weighted false allows
  • 90% context coverage; under 5% stale context cases

Kill test: no paid pilot, fewer than two platforms consuming attributes beyond basic classifications, or no material decision lift. Then narrow to context feeds, governance evidence, and storage-native controls.

Risks, each with a fallback

  • Platforms will not consume external context: shadow mode first, open schemas; fall back to precomputed context feeds.
  • Context is incomplete or substitutable: measure coverage and lift before committing; fall back to storage-native controls.
  • GTM complexity: specialist sellers, one package, local projections; fall back to an Enterprise Data Cloud feature.

Staged, evidence-gated, neutral: a bet Everpure can make without betting the company.
